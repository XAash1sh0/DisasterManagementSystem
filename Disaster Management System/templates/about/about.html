About Us

We are a team dedicated to enhancing disaster awareness and response through powerful, user-friendly visualization tools. Our platform provides up-to-date, interactive maps and filters to help users explore disaster events by type, location, and time.

By aggregating reliable data from trusted sources, we aim to empower communities, emergency responders, researchers, and policymakers with actionable insights. We believe that clear, accessible information is key to reducing disaster impacts and saving lives.

This project is driven by experts in GIS, data analysis, and emergency management, committed to continuous improvement and innovation.

We welcome your feedback and collaboration to make disaster data more useful and impactful.
